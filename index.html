How to Play SlotRPS

Objective: Reduce CPU HP to 0 before they reduce yours. Each win deals damage, each loss costs HP + coins.

Rules:

Rock beats Scissors | Paper beats Rock | Scissors beats Paper

Damage: Normal win = 20 HP | Boss round = 30 HP | Critical hit = 2x damage

Combo: 3+ wins in a row = x1.5 damage multiplier

Boss Rounds: Every 5th round, CPU is stronger and deals more damage!

History of Rock Paper Scissors

Origin: RPS originated in China during the Han Dynasty ~2000 years ago as "shoushiling" - hand command game.

Japan: Called "Jan-ken" and became popular in 17th century. Used by kids to settle disputes.

West: Brought to Europe/US in early 1900s. Now used worldwide for decision making and esports!

Fun Fact: There's a World Rock Paper Scissors Championship with $10,000 prize pool!
